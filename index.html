About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation describes itself as a community-driven nonprofit focused on long-term, practical impact. The organization highlights projects that address everyday needs while building dignity and opportunity in underserved communities.

Ongoing Projects

Project Bachpanshala

Education support for underprivileged children through learning initiatives and outreach.

📚

Project Seva

Community welfare support including food and clothing distribution for families in need.

🍲

Project Udaan

Women empowerment through opportunities, confidence-building, and skill pathways.

🕊️

Project Jeev

Animal welfare initiatives with focus on rescue, protection, and regular feeding.

🐾

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation efforts such as plantation drives and sustainability awareness.

🌱

Social Impact & Purpose

  • Improves access to education for children in vulnerable communities.
  • Supports basic needs like food, care, and essential social welfare services.
  • Encourages women-led growth and self-reliance through empowerment-focused programs.
  • Builds compassion for animals and environmental responsibility in society.
